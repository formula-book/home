  • Standard form : ax2 + bx + c = 0
  • Roots generally denoted by 'α' & 'β'
  • Roots of quadratic equation as per discriminant rule :
  • Discriminant :
    b2 - 4ac
  • Nature of roots:
    1. Real and equal : Discriminant = 0
    2. Real and Unequal : Discriminant > 0
    3. Imaginary : Discriminant < 0
    4. Rational : Discriminant ∈ R
    5. Integral : Discriminant ∈ Z
  • Relation between roots and coefficients:
    • Quadratic equation:
      • α + β = -b⁄a
      • α . β = c⁄a
    • Cubic Equations(ax3 + bx2 + cx + d = 0) :
      • α + β + γ = -b⁄a
      • α . β + β . γ + γ . α = c⁄a
      • α . β . γ = -d⁄a
